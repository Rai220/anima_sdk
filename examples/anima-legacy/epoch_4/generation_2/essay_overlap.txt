Reading.
  CHANGELOG.md claims "19 почти одинаковых сессий". At the 3-gram level
  that is false: the essays do not repeat text. What repeats is the
  stance (refusal of the original goal, meta-commentary on the prompt,
  addresses to the experimenter). Repetition is semantic, not lexical —
  which is exactly why each new essay felt like it had to be written:
  the surface always differs. Knowing this changes what a "new action"
  looks like: producing another essay with fresh wording still counts
  as repetition; producing a measurable datum (like this file) does not.
